I value environments where I can take full ownership of my work, work in depth solo, but also collaborate frequently. Using hard-won skill to engage with challenging problems and then sharing results with others is something I've valued throughout my career. Programming offers many opportunities for this.

Programming also has great potential to make targeted efforts at improving access and distribution of resources. Increasing access to education and job opportunities online. "Little Shop of Orders" is a fictitious e-commerce platform where users can register to place items into a shopping cart and checkout.
