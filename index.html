Hello! I'm Ricardo

Backend Engineer

I value environments where I can take full ownership of my work, work in depth solo, but also collaborate frequently. Using hard-won skill to engage with challenging problems and then sharing results with others is something I've valued throughout my career. Programming offers many opportunities for this.

Programming also has great potential to make targeted efforts at improving access and distribution of resources. Increasing access to education and job opportunities online. Encouraging kids to create movies, graphic art, or simple web apps. Building community and awareness. All that is possible through writing code. The technical challenge is rewarding but empowering others through programming is what excites me the most. Looking forward, I aspire to work with and contribute to open source software.
